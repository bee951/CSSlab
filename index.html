M6

An American heavy tank fro WWII

Development started in 1940 and was canceled in 1944. Main armament varied from a 75mm gun to a 105mm gun.

T29

An American heavy tank designed to counter King Tigers

The tank had 279mm thick turret armor and mounted a 105mm gun.

T32

An American heavy prototype tank from WWII

The T32 was designed to use many Pershing tank components but have heavier armor and mount a larger gun.

M103

Only operational American heavy tank

The tank was deployed during the Korean War and mounted a 120mm gun.
